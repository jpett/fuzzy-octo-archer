The Idea..

I was trying to think of a way of making a one page site that looked good and was good to use. That kept the feel of seperate pages.
Click the Arrow to move down a page or the tracker on the right to jump around.
Ignore the colors i picked them at random

Some Customisating

You Can use background pictures instead of colors. You might want to change the colors though...

And you can use loads of text on one page if you really wanted to.
